About the Club

  • Club was founded on the 22nd day of October, 2015, to raise awareness for the life of Magneto
  • The club actively has one member, the founder, Christopher Aben
  • The club is working on developing a lecture series on Magnetism and Genetics: (two areas of expertise for Magneto)
  • The club hopes to debate the actions of Magneto and determine whether he is truly a villain or not
